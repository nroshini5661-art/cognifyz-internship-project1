THE PROGRAM

What is the intership?

A structured, outcome-driven program designed to turn motivated learners into job-ready developers.

The Cognifyz Web Developer Internship is a competitive program where selected interns work alongside experienced engineers to build, ship, and maintain real-world web applications.

You won't be making coffee. You'll be writing code, reviewing PRs, attending standups, and contributing to products used by real users — from day one.

At the end of the program, you'll have a portfolio of completed projects, a certificate of completion, and a professional network that opens doors.
